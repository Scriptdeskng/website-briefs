# Code 11 Website Design Brief

## Objective

To position Code 11 as a leading infrastructure provider for VAS, telecom, and subscription-based digital services.

The website should clearly communicate that Code 11 builds and operates **revenue-generating infrastructure**, not just software.

It should attract serious clients (telcos, VAS providers, aggregators, enterprises) and convert them into conversations.

---

## Core Positioning

Code 11 builds and operates the infrastructure behind high-performing VAS and subscription platforms.

Alternative positioning:
- We turn VAS traffic into revenue through optimized infrastructure
- We build revenue-driven digital infrastructure for telecom and VAS companies

---

## Target Audience

- Telecom operators  
- VAS companies  
- Aggregators  
- Digital service providers  
- Gaming and lottery platforms  
- Enterprise clients  

---

## Tone & Style

- Confident and authoritative  
- Technical but easy to understand  
- Outcome-focused (revenue, performance, scale)  
- Modern, clean, minimal  
- No fluff, no generic agency language  

---

## Key Messaging Phrases

Use consistently across the site:

- Revenue-driven infrastructure  
- Subscription engine  
- Built for scale  
- Optimize. Scale. Monetize.  
- From traffic to revenue  
- High-performance VAS platforms  
- Battle-tested systems  
- Infrastructure that drives revenue  
- Beyond development — we build growth systems  

---

## Visual Direction

- Clean, modern UI  
- Dashboard-style visuals (analytics feel)  
- System diagrams and flows  
- Minimal stock imagery  
- Tech-forward color palette  
- Subtle animations  

---

## Design Guidelines

### Avoid:
- Generic “software company” visuals  
- Stock images of developers  
- Overly wordy sections  
- Buzzword-heavy content  

### Focus on:
- Performance  
- Data  
- Systems  
- Results  

The site should feel like a **platform company**, not an agency.

---

## Important Notes

- Do NOT include Intelli pricing on this website  
- Intelli should be presented as the underlying infrastructure, not the primary product  
- Emphasis should remain on outcomes: revenue, scale, performance  

---

## Final Goal

When a VAS company visits the website, they should immediately feel:

"This is the team that can improve our revenue and fix our infrastructure."

The website should drive one action:

Start a conversation with Code 11.
