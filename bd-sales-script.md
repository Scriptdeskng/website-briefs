# Code 11 — BDM Sales Script (VAS / Telecom)

## Objective

To help Code 11 Business Development Managers consistently generate, qualify, and close opportunities with telecom operators, VAS companies, aggregators, and digital service providers.

The focus is on selling **revenue infrastructure**, not just software development.

---

## 1. Opening (First Contact)

### Goal:
Start a conversation and establish relevance.

---

### Cold Call / Intro Message

**Option 1 (Direct):**
Hi [Name], I’m reaching out from Code 11.  
We work with VAS and telecom companies to improve subscription conversions and stabilize billing infrastructure.  

I wanted to understand how you currently manage your campaign tracking and subscription flows.

---

**Option 2 (Conversational):**
Hi [Name], I came across your VAS services and wanted to ask — how are you currently handling campaign performance tracking and subscription management?

---

## 2. Discovery

### Goal:
Understand their current system and identify problems.

---

### Key Questions

- How do you currently track campaign performance?
- What channels do you use (SMS, USSD, API, Web)?
- Do you have visibility into conversion rates?
- How reliable is your billing and renewal system?
- Do you experience drop-offs during subscription?

---

### Insight Statement

We see this often — most VAS platforms generate traffic but lose revenue due to weak infrastructure.

---

## 3. Positioning Code 11

### Goal:
Explain what Code 11 does clearly.

---

At Code 11, we build and operate the infrastructure behind VAS platforms — from campaign tracking to subscription billing and analytics.

So instead of just generating traffic, you convert and retain users more effectively.

---

## 4. Value Alignment

### Goal:
Tie your solution to their problem.

---

**If conversion is low:**
We optimize campaign flows and tracking so you can see where users drop off and improve conversions.

**If billing is unreliable:**
We implement more reliable subscription systems with retry logic to reduce revenue loss.

---

### Core Message

Our goal is simple — improve your revenue without increasing your traffic.

---

## 5. Introducing Intelli

### Goal:
Anchor your infrastructure advantage.

---

We’ve built an internal infrastructure called Intelli that powers campaign tracking, subscriptions, and integrations.

This allows us to deploy faster and continuously optimize performance.

---

## 6. Call to Action

### Goal:
Secure a meeting/demo.

---

I think it would make sense to walk you through how we approach this and identify where improvements can be made.

Would you be open to a quick session this week?

---

## 7. Meeting Structure

- Understand current system
- Identify inefficiencies
- Show improvement areas
- Propose solution approach

---

### Closing Line

Based on what we’ve seen, there’s clear room to improve your conversion and billing reliability.

We can deploy a system that handles this end-to-end and continuously optimize it.

---

## 8. Pricing Handling

If asked early:

Pricing depends on your current setup and scale.  
We typically structure it as deployment plus ongoing optimization.

Once we understand your system, we’ll propose something aligned with your goals.

---

## 9. Follow-Up Message

Hi [Name], great speaking earlier.  

Based on our discussion, we see clear opportunities to improve your campaign performance and subscription flow.  

We’ll share a structured approach shortly.

---

## 10. Key Selling Principles

- Sell outcomes, not software  
- Focus on revenue improvement  
- Ask questions before pitching  
- Position Code 11 as a long-term partner  

---

## 11. Power Phrases

- Improve conversion without increasing traffic  
- Revenue leakage  
- Infrastructure for scale  
- Subscription optimization  
- We don’t just build — we operate  

---

## 12. Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Talking too much about technical details  
- Pitching too early  
- Being generic  
- Not understanding the client’s system  

---

## Final Note

Code 11 is not a development agency.

Code 11 is a **revenue infrastructure partner for VAS and telecom businesses**.
