# Job Description — Business Development Manager (BDM)
## Code 11

---

## Role Overview

The Business Development Manager (BDM) will be responsible for driving revenue growth for Code 11 by identifying, engaging, and closing opportunities within the telecom, VAS, and enterprise sectors.

The BDM will position Code 11 as a provider of **revenue-driven infrastructure**, not just software development services.

---

## Key Objective

Generate and close high-value deals by connecting Code 11’s infrastructure capabilities with real business problems in the market.

---

## Target Industries

- Telecom operators  
- VAS companies  
- Aggregators  
- Digital service providers  
- Gaming and lottery platforms  
- Enterprise clients  

---

## Core Responsibilities

### 1. Lead Generation

- Identify and engage potential clients in target industries  
- Build and maintain a pipeline of qualified prospects  
- Leverage industry relationships and networks  

---

### 2. Client Engagement

- Initiate conversations with decision-makers  
- Conduct discovery sessions to understand client needs  
- Identify pain points related to VAS infrastructure and revenue performance  

---

### 3. Solution Positioning

- Clearly articulate Code 11’s offerings  
- Position Code 11 as a revenue infrastructure partner  
- Translate technical capabilities into business value  

---

### 4. Sales Execution

- Drive opportunities from initial contact to close  
- Collaborate with technical team for proposals  
- Participate in presentations and demos  

---

### 5. Deal Closing

- Negotiate commercial terms  
- Close contracts and agreements  
- Ensure alignment with company goals  

---

### 6. Relationship Management

- Maintain strong relationships with clients  
- Identify upsell and expansion opportunities  
- Ensure long-term engagement  

---

### 7. Market Intelligence

- Monitor industry trends and competitor activity  
- Provide feedback to leadership on market opportunities  

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Number of qualified leads generated  
- Number of client meetings conducted  
- Number of proposals submitted  
- Deals closed (monthly/quarterly)  
- Total revenue generated  
- Pipeline value  

---

## Required Skills & Experience

- Proven experience in business development or sales  
- Experience working with telecom, VAS, fintech, or enterprise clients  
- Strong understanding of technology-driven solutions  
- Ability to communicate complex ideas simply  
- Strong negotiation and closing skills  
- Existing industry relationships (preferred)  

---

## Preferred Background

- Experience selling infrastructure, SaaS, or technical products  
- Experience working with telcos or aggregators  
- Familiarity with subscription-based systems  

---

## Compensation Structure

- Base Salary:TBD  
- Commission: 5% – 10% of closed deals  

---

## Success Profile

The ideal candidate:

- Thinks in terms of **business value, not just sales activity**  
- Is comfortable speaking with technical and non-technical stakeholders  
- Is driven by results and revenue  
- Can independently source and close deals  

---

## What Success Looks Like

Within 3–6 months:

- A strong pipeline of qualified opportunities  
- Multiple client engagements and proposals  
- At least 1–2 closed deals  

---

## Final Note

This role is critical to Code 11’s growth.

The BDM is not just selling services —  
they are driving the adoption of **revenue infrastructure across the VAS and telecom ecosystem**.
